Cowboy Coffee

Cowboy coffee got its name from the cowboys cooking coffee by the campfire. Its not fancy but it gets the job done. All you need is fire, coffee, water and a pot.

Ingredients

  • A pot
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of ground coffee
  • 4 cups (1 quart) of water
  • About half a cup of cold water
  • A heat source

Steps

  1. Add your coffee to your pot of water.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Let it boil until the coffee grounds sink to the bottom, it takes about 3 or 4 minutes.
  4. Remove it from the heat source and set it aside so the grounds can settle to the bottom. Let it sit for about 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  5. Pour in a cup.
  6. Drink!

Be careful it'll be hot!
